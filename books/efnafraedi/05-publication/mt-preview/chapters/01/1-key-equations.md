### Jöfnur

$$\text{density} = \frac{\text{mass}}{\text{volume}}$$

$${T}_{\text{°C}} = \frac{5}{9} \times ({T}_{\text{°F}} - 32)$$

$${T}_{\text{°F}} = (\frac{9}{5} \times {T}_{\text{°C}}) + 32$$

$${T}_{\text{K}} = \text{°C} + 273.15$$

$${T}_{\text{°C}} = \text{K} - 273.15$$
