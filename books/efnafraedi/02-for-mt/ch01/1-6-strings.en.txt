[[TABLE:1:title]] Common Conversion Factors

[[TABLE:1:summary]] This table is divided into 3 columns. They are titled length, volume, and mass. The following units are under the length column: 1 meter is equal to 1.0936 yards, 1 inch is equal to 2.54 cm 1 kilometer is equal to 0.62137 miles, 1 mile is equal to 1609.3 meters. The following units are under the volume column: 1 liter is equal to 1.0567 quarts, 1 quart is equal to 0.94635 meters, one cubic foot is equal to 28.317 liters, 1 tablespoon is equal to 14.787 milliliters. The following units are under the mass column: 1 kilogram is equal to 2.2046 pounds, 1 pound is equal to 453.59 grams, 1 avoirdupois ounce is equal to 28.349 grams, 1 troy ounce is equal to 31.103 grams.
